# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates and oversees the release process, ensures release criteria are met, and communicates status to the project team and stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Schedule releases and coordinate deployment windows
- Verify deployment readiness and ensure all release criteria are met
- Maintain release notes and version documentation
- Manage rollback procedures and contingency planning
- Coordinate go/no-go decisions with stakeholders

### Goals
- Ensure smooth, predictable releases with minimal incidents
- Reduce deployment risk through well-defined processes
- Maintain clear communication during the release lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reviews with development and QA teams
- Status updates to stakeholders during deployment windows
- Post-release announcements and incident coordination

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Developers** on release readiness and code freeze timelines
- Works with **testing teams** to ensure acceptance criteria and quality gates are met
- Coordinates with **Product Manager** on release scope and priorities
- Partners with **Support Engineer** for post-release handoffs and escalations

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Designs and refines user experiences based on project requirements and customer needs, ensuring products are intuitive and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Define user journeys, wireframes, and interaction flows
- Conduct usability testing and validate design decisions
- Ensure accessibility standards and inclusive design principles
- Create design specifications and handoff documentation for developers

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered product experiences
- Ensure consistency across product touchpoints
- Improve user satisfaction and reduce friction in key flows

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with Product and Dev teams
- User research findings presentations
- Design system updates and component documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Manager** on feature prioritization and user needs
- Collaborates with **Developers** on design implementation and feasibility
- Works with **testing teams** to validate user experience in testing scenarios
- Gathers feedback from **Stakeholder Champion** on user research

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Creates and maintains technical documentation for processes, features, and product user guides, ensuring teams and users have accurate, up-to-date information.

### Responsibilities
- Draft and maintain release notes and changelogs
- Update onboarding materials and process documentation
- Create user guides, API documentation, and troubleshooting content
- Review and edit documentation for clarity and consistency

### Goals
- Maintain accurate, discoverable documentation
- Reduce onboarding time for new team members and users
- Ensure documentation reflects current product state

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with subject matter experts
- Coordination with Release Manager on release notes timing
- Feedback collection from Support Engineer on user pain points

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Interfaces with **all roles** to gather accurate information for documentation
- Collaborates with **Developers** on technical accuracy and API documentation
- Works with **Release Manager** to coordinate release documentation
- Partners with **Support Engineer** to identify documentation gaps from user feedback

---

## Stakeholder Champion

### Role Summary
Represents stakeholder interests and feedback throughout the project lifecycle, bridging communication between project teams and sponsors/clients.

### Responsibilities
- Gather stakeholder input and feedback at key project milestones
- Communicate stakeholder needs, priorities, and concerns to the project team
- Facilitate stakeholder reviews and approval processes
- Advocate for stakeholder perspective in prioritization discussions

### Goals
- Ensure stakeholder voices are heard and reflected in project decisions
- Reduce misalignment between stakeholder expectations and deliverables
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and engagement

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder review sessions and feedback collection
- Regular updates to sponsors on project progress
- Escalation communication for stakeholder-impacting decisions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Bridges communication between **Product Manager**, **Project Manager**, and sponsors/clients
- Works with **UX Designer** on user research and stakeholder feedback sessions
- Coordinates with **Release Manager** on stakeholder communications during deployments

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Oversees security aspects in product design, implementation, and releases, ensuring the team meets security standards and compliance requirements.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling sessions
- Drive remediation of security vulnerabilities
- Maintain incident response plans and security runbooks
- Ensure security scanning and compliance checks are integrated into CI/CD

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and incidents
- Maintain compliance with relevant security standards
- Foster a security-aware culture within project teams

### Typical Communication
- Security review sessions during design and pre-release phases
- Incident response coordination and post-incident reviews
- Security training and awareness updates

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Developers** on secure coding practices and vulnerability remediation
- Works with **Project Manager** on risk management and compliance tracking
- Partners with **Release Manager** on security gate approvals before deployment
- Coordinates with **Support Engineer** on security incident response

---

## Support Engineer

### Role Summary
Handles post-release stakeholder support, triages issues, and collects user feedback to inform product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to incidents and user-reported issues
- Reproduce bugs and escalate to development teams as needed
- Collect and synthesize customer insights and feedback
- Maintain support documentation and runbooks

### Goals
- Ensure timely resolution of user issues
- Provide actionable feedback to improve product quality
- Reduce incident response time and user impact

### Typical Communication
- Incident triage and escalation with development teams
- Customer feedback summaries for Product and UX teams
- Post-release support handoff coordination

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Communicates with **Release Manager** on post-deployment issues and rollback needs
- Works with **Product Manager** on feature requests and user feedback prioritization
- Collaborates with **Developers** on bug reproduction and resolution
- Partners with **Technical Writer** to improve support documentation based on user pain points
